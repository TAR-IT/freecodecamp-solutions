Briston Maroney

Briston Maroney performing live.

Briston Maroney is an American indie rock singer-songwriter originally from Knoxville, Tennessee. He gained popularity after appearing on the reality television series American Idol. Maroney's music is characterized by his distinctive voice, introspective lyrics, and energetic performances.

His debut album "Sunflower" released in 2021 received critical acclaim for its raw emotion and authenticity. Maroney continues to captivate audiences with his heartfelt music and compelling performances.
